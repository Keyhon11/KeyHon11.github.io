Evergreen Secondary School

I was a student in Evergreen Secondary School from 2015-2019. I was part of the Prefectorial Board and a member of Cross Country.

I have had many fond memories in the school and I will share some of my experiences here!

My EXCO Batch

3 things I have learnt as a Welfare EXCO

  1. People would rather speak with someone they know than go to a counselor.
  2. Sharing with others small things such as food can go a long way.
  3. You never know what people have gone through, so do not jump to conclusions.

Events I participated/planned

Family day (2018)

I helped in the planning held for 92 prefects. The objective was to bond the whole prefect team so we will be able to work better with each other in future events.

Chinese New Year Hamper Wrapping (2018)

I helped with the packing of the hampers. We delivered the hampers to needy families around the area.

Secondary One Orientation (2018 & 2019)

I helped with the execution of the event. I was the facilitator in charge of my assigned class.

Xi'an Trip 2018

3 things I have learnt during the trip

  1. Chinese Paper Cutting
  2. Chinese Opera Mask Making
  3. History of the Terracotta Warriors
